MR & MS POSTURE INTERNATIONAL

Mr. and Ms. Posture International is an unconventional pageant that aims to create awareness about alternative health care and natural lifestyle. It also aims to touch more lives and to create a movement that makes a significant difference in the community by teaching, informing and engaging people to

“THINK, EAT, MOVE and STAND RIGHT.”
The unique concept enables men and women to become a contemporary ambassador of alternative healthcare and natural healthy lifestyles which will help to create positive change in the community. It is said that a healthy society ignites people and nation to economic prosperity and a happier life. The pageant is distinctive given that it is the sole competition that focuses on natural healthy lifestyles and alternative health treatment such as chiropractic. Another factor that sets it apart from the rest is, the pageant has an Essay Round


ABOUT CHIROPRACTIC DIPLOMATIC CORPS INC.



The Chiropractic Diplomatic Corps (CDC) was founded in 1997 by a small group of committed chiropractors who wanted to see chiropractic grow across the world. During its 25th anniversary in 2022, the CDC- Chiro launched its signature event which is the Mr. & Ms. Posture Philippines. The success of 2022 and 2023 edition fueled the Mr. & Ms. Posture International.



Our primary aim in 2020 and beyond is to encourage the development of new chiropractic institutions, beginning in the Philippines, India, and the Far East. This non-profit organization is founded by Michel “docMIKE” Tetrault, Doctor of Chiropractic. Chiropractic Diplomatic Corps, Inc. Canadian Not-for-profit Corporation Registration No. 421665-2 Philippines branch office, registration No. 202100009



VISION

To see the growth of the Chiropractic profession reach 90% in global access to Chiropractic services by the year 2095, Chiropractic’s Bi-Centennial Year.



MISSION

The CHIROPRACTIC DIPLOMATIC CORPS is a Canadian nonprofit non-government Chiropractic multi-national and humanitarian organization interested in the development of chiropractic education at International standards throughout the world, as set out in the organization’s objectives and guidelines.
