ABOUT CHIROPRACTIC DIPLOMATIC CORPS INC.

The Chiropractic Diplomatic Corps (CDC) was founded in 1997 by a small group of committed chiropractors who wanted to see chiropractic grow across the world. During its 25th anniversary in 2022, the CDC- Chiro launched its signature event which is the Mr. & Ms. Posture Philippines. The success of 2022 and 2023 edition fueled the Mr. & Ms. Posture International.

Our primary aim in 2020 and beyond is to encourage the development of new chiropractic institutions, beginning in the Philippines, India, and the Far East.

This non-profit organization is founded by Michel “docMIKE” Tetrault, Doctor of Chiropractic. Chiropractic Diplomatic Corps, Inc.
